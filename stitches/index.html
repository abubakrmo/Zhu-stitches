About us

Zhu Stitches is a consortium with the goal of meeting human needs in every sphere of life. Zhu Stitches is a dynamic fashion house led by a visionary Nigerian fashion consultant, Zhulayqoh. Our company values quality and perfection. Do you have your own design you'd like to make into an outfit? Can't find any design you'd like or do not have any design in mind? Not to worry, we have your back. We offer the cafeteria approach from fabric selection to design selection and outfit Production. We are able to interpret your design and make it into that fabulous outfit you desire. If you don't have any design we are able to make suggestions for you.
